# Secondary research: Supply chain visibility tooling adoption

- **Source**: Industry analyst report on supply chain visibility tooling
- **Link**: (internal research library reference)
- **Date published**: 2026-03-15

## Key finding
Mid-size manufacturers (500-5000 employees) report spending an average of
6-9 hours per week per planner on manual data reconciliation across ERP and
planning systems, with the majority citing "trust in automated alerts" as
the top reason for not adopting existing visibility tools.

## Relevance
Directly supports the problem statement's time-cost claim and explains why
past automated tools may have failed to stick for this segment - trust in
alert quality matters as much as the underlying detection capability.
