# Vision: PulseBoard

## Problem Statement
Supply chain planners at mid-size manufacturers spend 8-10 hours a week manually
reconciling demand forecasts across disconnected spreadsheets and ERP exports,
discovering stockout risk only after it has already affected a customer order.
There is no single place that surfaces "what's about to go wrong" early enough
to act on it.

## Target Users
- Demand planners at manufacturers with 3-10 distribution centers
- Supply chain ops managers who own the weekly S&OP review

## Vision Statement
PulseBoard becomes the early-warning system every planner checks before their
first coffee - surfacing the three supply risks that matter most today, in
plain language, before they become customer-facing problems.

## North Star Metric
Reduction in stockout-driven expedite shipments per quarter, measured per
customer account.

## Non-Goals
- We are not building a full ERP replacement
- We are not optimizing inbound freight routing in v1
